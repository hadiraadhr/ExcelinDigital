HOW AN IGNITION RUNS

Domain experts. Five stages. Six weeks.

We bring in a senior expert in your exact challenge — we also run AI natively across every stage of the work. That combination means faster delivery and sharper output. Yet we don't just deliver and leave. Throughout the engagement, your expert builds AI tools and assets — tailored to your context. At exit, those tools transfer to your team. And your expert stays reachable — ready to support during execution

01 · Stress-test the Challenge We interview your team and dive deep into your data — to confirm what's real, what's assumed, and where the blind spots are Out · Diagnonsis Report
02 · Prioritized Recommendations AI agents handle the analysis heavy lifting — Our domain experts focus on what matters: judgment and recommendations built to tier-1 rigor. Out · Ranked Recommendations
03 · Subject Matter Upskilling Fluency built in context, not in a classroom. We tailor learning sessions to your domain woven into the live work — your team practices applying AI tools to the actual decisions, not hypothetical ones Out · Leaders Fluency
04 · AI Artifacts Every agent, tool, and trained skill our expert used during the engagement is packaged and handed over — ready for your team to run independently. Out · Trained AI Agents / Skills
05 · Execution-Ready A KPI framework and a clear execution brief — plus the AI tools. Your domain expert stays on call to unblock what comes next. Out · Execution Framework
